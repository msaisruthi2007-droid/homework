Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, better known as MS Dhoni, is an Indian former professional cricketer who was captain of the Indian national team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2016 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014. Known for his calm demeanor under pressure and exceptional finishing skills, Dhoni is widely regarded as one of the greatest captains and wicket-keeper batsmen in the history of cricket. Under his leadership, India won the 2007 ICC World Twenty20, the 2010 and 2016 Asia Cups, the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy.
